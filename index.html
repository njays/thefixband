ABOUT

Looking for a band that’s lively, high quality and vibrant? The Fix can provide you with live music that promises to entertain and get you on your feet. Based in Cardiff, The Fix play exciting arrangements of current music, performing them with style and energy. We are a 5-8-piece band comprising some of the best UK musicians who have been playing together for many years.
Our line up is as follows:
8 piece: Drums, Bass, Guitar, Keys, Sax, Trumpet, Trombone and Singer (a smaller band is negotiable consisting of Drums, Bass, Guitar, Keys, Sax and Singer)

WEDDINGS/EVENTS

Whatever the occasion, The Fix has got you covered. We’ll get you up and dancing to all the songs you love to sing along to. For more information on our set list click here. Whether you’re looking for music for your special day, a band to get your party started or something that blends more into the background, The Fix can provide it for you. Here’s what else we can offer:

  • Singer & Piano
  • Jazz Sax & Piano
  • Jazz Trio
  • Jazz Quartet
  • Jazz Quintet
  • DJ Service
  • Sax and DJ Service

Be sure to contact us about your special day, and we will be with you every step of the way to make sure your day is all about you!
