FAQ

How much does it cost to book The Fix?

The costs depend on a number of factors including location, travel times, the length of time we play and how many of us you want to play. To find out how much it will cost click herehere and let us know your requirements so we can provide you with a quote.

Can I come and see the band at a show before I book?

We normally play private performances. However you can watch some videos and you can see our references. Click here for more.

How much deposit do you require?

When you book with The Fix we don’t ask for a deposit, instead when our quote is agreed, a signed contract will be sent to you that acts as a deposit. We do expect payment on the night/24 hours in advance of the performance however.

How far do you travel?

We are based in Cardiff but are happy to travel anywhere if the travel costs are covered.

How long do you take to set up and set down?

We normally take 60 to 90 minutes to set up. We don’t want to rush setting up as we need to make sure everything is ready and perfect for our performance. Setting down takes us 45mins, and will be done directly after the performance. We can provide a DJ after the set to keep the dance floor moving, this will come at an extra cost.

What happens if I need to cancel?

All the cancellation procedures are included in the contract that will be sent to you after booking.

Will you take requests?

We love to take requests! Please provide these well before the performance so we can perfect the songs to make your event special for you, especially for the first dance.
